Ingredients:
- 1 bag of spaghetti
- 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
- lots of olive oil, parmesan chese, salt, pepper, some garlic powder

Directions:
  1. Boil a bunch of water in pot
  2. Cook spaghetti until aldente (10m)
  3. Warm-up sauce
  4. Drain and rinse noodles
  5. Add olive oil, parmesan chese, salt, pepper, some garlic powder
  6. Serve noodles and put sauce on noodles.
